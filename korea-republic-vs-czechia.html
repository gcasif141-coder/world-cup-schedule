1. Match Overview & Historical Clashes

Korea Republic (South Korea) returns to the World Cup stage with a talented squad led by global icon Son Heung-min and defensive titan Kim Min-jae. Known for their fast transition and tireless work rate, the Taeguk Warriors are targeting a deep knockout phase run.

Czechia (Czech Republic), boasting physical robustness and organized defense anchored by skipper Tomáš Souček, poses a formidable challenge. With the prolific Patrik Schick leading the frontline, the Czechs will attempt to neutralize South Korea's speed and control the tempo through central midfield dominance.

6. Tactical Battle & Key Players

The central clash lies between Lee Kang-in's playmaker creativity and Czechia's defensive midfielder Tomáš Souček. If Souček can close down Lee's passing lanes, the service to Son Heung-min and Hwang Hee-chan will be severely limited.

Conversely, Kim Min-jae will be tasked with marking Leverkusen's star striker Patrik Schick. Schick is lethal in aerial duels and positioning, meaning Kim's defensive anticipation will be tested to the absolute limit.
